Adam is Chief Investment Officer at Princeton Asset Management, where he has directed investment strategy and policy since 2008. He brings 15+ years of institutional investment experience, including roles at PNC Wealth Management, where he served on the Investment Committee of a $120+ billion AUM institution, and as Chief Investment Officer of a billion-dollar single-family office.

Adam has a proven track record building and managing multi-asset portfolios across equities, fixed income, real estate, hedge funds, and private markets.

He holds an MBA in Finance from the University of Chicago, a JD Degree from New York Law School and a BA from Bowdoin College, where he graduated Summa Cum Laude. Adam is a CFA charterholder and a national speaker on investment strategy and risk management.
