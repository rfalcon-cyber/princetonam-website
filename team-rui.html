Rui is the Co-Founder and CEO of Princeton Asset Management, where she has led the firm since 2009. She brings over 20 years of investment management experience, across equities, fixed income, alternatives, and private markets.

Earlier in her career, she held senior roles at J.P. Morgan Securities Debt Capital Markets, Fannie Mae Treasury and Fixed Income, and served as Director of Investment Strategy for a large single-family office.

Rui holds an MBA in Finance and Accounting from the University of Chicago and a BA from Hunan University. She is a CFA charterholder and has earned the trust of families, trusts, foundations, and institutional clients through her expertise in portfolio construction, risk management, and tailored wealth advisory.

Rui is active in local charity work with various organizations including Chinese Association of Science, Education and Culture.
